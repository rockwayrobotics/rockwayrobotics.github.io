About us

We’re Rockway Robotics, FRC Team 8089. We’re from Rockway Mennonite Collegiate in Kitchener, Ontario. You can learn more about Rockway Mennonite Collegiate here. Our Robotics team is a place for letting students explore STEM and encourage their curiosity. The FRC competition is where our students use the skills they’ve developed to compete in a high-stakes competition. The Robotics team is a unique opportunity to learn alongside professional mentors, who aid the team providing valuable knowledge and a look into a potential future in STEM.

Build Wonder. Build Respect. Build Bridges. Build Robots.
